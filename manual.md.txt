This TipCalculator require API 21 or above.

User has to enter a valid checkAmount and the partySize to run the app,
Or a Toast error message will pop up saying you must enter a valid input.

Once the ComputeButton is clicked, the user will see the amount of tips
each person has to pay in the party. 

The total amount is the amount of the check divided by the party size then
plus the amount of tips each person is responsible for (Rounded to the nearest integer).


The App will display the total amount in 15%, 20% and 25%.
